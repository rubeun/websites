Burn The TV Live Music Event Streaming

Live Music Broadcast

Burn The TV was born from the desire to share the sounds and sights of San Francisco's vibrant musical scene with the nation and the world

Broadcasting regularly from local clubs like Monarch (Awarded Best Sound by the SF Bay Guardian & SF Weekly as well as Named Top 10 Best Sound Systems in the US by Beatport), Burn The TV is San Francisco's local version of music streaming services like Be-at.tv, Boiler Room and Mixmag

Full Event Broadcast Archive

In the growing VOD (Video On Demand) centric world we live in, Burn The TV provides full recordings of previous broadcasts ready for viewing the day after the show

Those unable to attend event for whatever reason or fans from remote locations or different countries will have full access to shows on popular sites like YouTube

Partnering with many talented musicians and DJs in San Francisco, Burn The TV is a small but growing community

Web Development

Rubeun Tan Web Services is a all-purpose, platform-independent digital solution service

Primarily focused on web development using HTML, CSS, JQuery, PHP, MySQL as well as popular and highly popular CMS like Wordpress.

Live Web Streaming

Rubeun Tan Web Services also handles periphery solutions for anything web related from live web broadcasts utilising live video streaming services like UStream and San Francisco local company DaCast to deliver content.

With fairly compact broadcasting equipment, multi-camera shots are possible to broadcast in SD and HD

Once broadcast, digital copies of the full broadcast are available to be uploaded to a video service of your choosing (YouTube, Vimeo, etc.)
